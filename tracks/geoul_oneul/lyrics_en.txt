A day goes by, the breathless hours drift away
the air of this house, once loud and sharp,
now a cold morning gone silent and heavy
Sweeping the clean house again without meaning to,
I stop at the mirror and look into my own eyes,
and the tears begin to fall
Yesterday I let you go for the last time, and it began—
I believed today would be kinder than the day before,
yet in the mirror today my eyes still only look sad
Love always looks so beautiful,
love always sets the heart fluttering—
then parting always leaves you this small and shabby,
parting always leaves you longing and sad like this
Tipping a glass of soju, remembering, missing you,
the ache returns and I'm sad all over again
Tomorrow at the mirror I'll shine brighter than today,
tomorrow at the mirror I'll be lovelier than today,
I vow it, tipping one more glass of soju,
aching for myself, aching for you, again
